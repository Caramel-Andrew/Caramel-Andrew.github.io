Taking block-breaking fun to the next level.

If you thought block breaking puzzle games couldn't get any more exciting, think again!
Tennis Breaker has taken the classic game to a whole new level.
With a giant board, a real tennis racket, and a real tennis ball,
this game will have your members working up a sweat as they fight for the highest score!

Play the classic game with a new sporting twist!

Use a real Tennis Racket and a real Tennis Ball to hit
blocks on the giant screen. If the block si touching one
or more of the seame color, it will be destroyed!
The larger the group, the higher the score!

Projected Screen

The game screen is projected onto any blank wall giving you lots of room to play.

Touch Sensor

Using an innovative distance and position sensor, Tennis Breaker recognizes the position of each ball hit and translates them to the play screen. With a range of up to 10 meters!

Customization

The same system can be adapted for a variety of sports such as football or baseball as well as tennis. Consultations are also available for non-sport use.
